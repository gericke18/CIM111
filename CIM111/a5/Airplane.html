Paper Airplane

Step 1

Fold a sheet of paper in half widthwise.

Step 2

Fold the upper corners of the paper in so they meet at the middle crease. The top of each fold will start a small way out from the middle crease â€“ there will be around the width of three or four fingers space on either side of the crease.

Step 3

Fold the top of the plane down so that it meets at the same point as the two wing tips.

Step 4

Fold a margin on each wing. The margin will be parallel to the line of the wing. The tips of the two margins will meet at the middle crease.

Step 5

Fold the plane in half.

Step 6

<

Now it is time to fold the wings. Each wing will be parallel to the bottom of the plane. The crease will start at the top of the nose of the plane (just where it starts to angle back).

Step 7

Open the wings up and that’s it! One Delta Wing ready for service!

Airplanes are fun.

They are also fragile.
